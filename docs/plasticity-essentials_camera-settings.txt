Camera settings are located on the right side of the screen.
Camera Settings
FOV:
Adjusts the camera's Field of View (FOV). The default value is 50 in perspective mode - lower values create a narrower view, while higher values expand it.
Squish:
Controls optical distortion by compressing the camera view. The default value is 1.0.
