Basic Usage
A Nominal Surface is a feature that heals holes on a surface area by generating additional spans in a hidden manner when working with visible surface CVs, allowing easy direct surface editing. The Remove Nominal Surface command displays the additional hidden spans on the surface area.
Select a face you want to reveal hidden CVs.
Use the
Remove Nominal Surface
command in the Command Palette.
